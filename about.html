About Us

More About Our TEAM

At Loomis, we bring together backgrounds from different fields, with a focus on automation through software, to simplify you business processes. Having known each other for over 10 years, our team knows how to work together efficiently and more nimbly than most software teams.

The different perspectives and professional experiences that we have had allow us to approach a problem from several directions and arrive at the best solution using first principles thinking.



It is hard to find a team that compliments each other well and allows for a real merit based thought process. Our team does just that, which encourages an honest look at the right solution for each client.

While our skills are broad, we believe there is no one out there who will offer more thorough solutions. Our skills include:

  • Software Management
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Machine Learning and Automation
  • Financial Modeling and Cost Impact Analysis
  • Enterprise Risk Management


Why Choose Us?

Most business people don't understand software. And most software people don't have an honest appreciation for business needs. We on the other hand, know these two worlds need to work together. And we know how to make that happen. Our three part process is sure to help you discover opportunities for efficiency, implement clever and maintainable solutions, and work with you to keep software solutions working for the long run.

Our Solution

Discovery

First, we will meet and discuss current business processes that you would like to improve. We will leverage our insight to figure out what solutions are available that you might not be aware of.

Implementation

We will quickly iterate and implement solutions. We pride ourselves on communication during this process to make sure the product is exactly what you are looking for.

Monitoring

We know our clients are often not software experts. Our automation solutions are designed to be understood and used by non-technical business owners. We will work with you to make sure the software we build works now and in the future.
